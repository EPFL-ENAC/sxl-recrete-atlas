## Welcome on ARC - the Atlas of Reused Concrete
 
ARC is an interactive database and website on concrete structural reuse. It presents and gathers data from over 50 projects built with concrete reused structurally.
 
Enter the website and explore the projects using the :mdi-map-outline: map view, the :mdi-grid: grid view, or the :mdi-list-box: list view.

To learn more about the atlas, click on the “:mdi-information:” button. A FAQ on concrete reuse is accessible under the ":mdi-crane:" button.
